About Dengue Fever

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection causing flu-like illness. Severe dengue can be life-threatening. Early detection and proper care lower fatality rates.

Prevention Tips

  • Use mosquito nets and repellents
  • Remove standing water
  • Wear long-sleeved clothing
  • Seek medical attention if symptoms appear
